KICH BAN THUYET TRINH DU AN: NEN TANG QUYEN GOP TU THIEN
Thoi luong du kien: 45 - 60 phut
Nguoi thuc hien: Tran Nguyen Phuong

PHAN 1: GIOI THIEU DU AN VA LY DO CHON DE TAI (2 - 3 phut)

Kinh thua Hoi dong va cac thay co, em ten la Tran Nguyen Phuong. Hom nay em xin phep trinh bay ve du an cuoi khoa cua minh mang ten: Website Quyen Gop Tu Thien.

Ly do em thuc hien du an nay xuat phat tu thuc te: hien nay cac hoat dong thien nguyen dang ngay cay pho bien, nhung viec ket noi giua nha hao tam va cac to chuc thien nguyen van con ton tai nhieu bat cap ve tinh minh bach va quy trinh xac nhan. Chinh vi vay, em muon xay dung mot nen tang cong nghe tin cay, noi moi giao dich deu duoc ghi nhan ro rang, giup nha hao tam de dang theo doi dong tien cua minh va giup Admin quan ly cac chien dich thien nguyen mot cach khoa hoc.

PHAN 2: PHAM VI VA DOI TUONG SU DUNG (2 - 3 phut)

Du an nay tap trung vao viec quan ly luong quyen gop tu khi bat dau chien dich cho den khi tien den tay nguoi can giup do. He thong chia thanh 3 doi tuong su dung chinh:

1. Khach vang lai (Guest): Day la nhung nguoi chua co tai khoan. Ho co the xem danh sach, chi tiet chien dich va thuc hien quyen gop ngay lap tuc sau khi cung cap thong tin dinh danh co ban.
2. Thanh vien (User): Day la nhung nguoi da dang ky. Ngoai cac quyen nu Guest, ho co the theo doi chien dich de nhan thong bao qua email, xem lich su quyen gop ca nhan va quan ly ho so.
3. Quan tri vien (Admin): Co toan quyen dieu hanh he thong, bao gom quan ly nguoi dung, tao moi va cap nhat chien dich, dac biet la nhiem vu doi soat va xac nhan cac giao dich tien te.

PHAN 3: PHAN TICH YEU CAU (5 phut)

Ve yeu cau chuc nang, he thong tap trung vao 3 nhom chinh:
- Nhom xac thuc: Dang ky, dang nhap va dac biet la tich hop dang nhap qua Google.
- Nhom tuong tac: Xem danh sach, chi tiet chien dich, thuc hien quyen gop va theo doi chien dich.
- Nhom quan tri: Quan ly User (khoa/mo khoa), quan ly Campaign (tao/sua/xoa) va quan ly Donation (phe duyet/tu choi).

Ve yeu cau phi chuc nang, em dat ra 5 tieu chi hang dau:
- Bao mat: Mat khau phai duoc ma hoa, phan quyen truy cap chat che.
- Tinh nhat quan: Du lieu tien te phai chinh xac tuyet doi, khong sai lech khi nhieu nguoi cung quyen gop.
- Usability: Giao dien phai don gian, de su dung tren ca may tinh va dien thoai.
- Tinh mo rong: Cau truc code phai cho phep de dang them cac tinh nang moi nhu cong thanh toan truc tuyen sau nay.

PHAN 4: USE CASE TONG QUAT (3 - 5 phut)

Dua tren phan tich tren, em thiet ke so do Use Case tong quat chia lam 2 phan:

Voi nguoi dung cuoi (Guest/User):
- Xem danh sach va chi tiet chien dich de tim hieu thong tin.
- Thuc hien quyen gop (chuc nang cot loi).
- Theo doi chien dich de cap nhat tinh hinh.
- Xem lich su de biet tien cua minh da duoc Admin xac nhan hay chua.

Voi Quan tri vien (Admin):
- Quan ly nguoi dung de ngan chan cac tai khoan spam hoac vi pham.
- Quan ly chien dich: Tu khau len y tuong, dat muc tieu den khi dong chien dich.
- Xac nhan quyen gop: Day la buoc quan trong nhat de hop le hoa dong tien vao quy chien dich.

PHAN 5: THIET KE KIEN TRUC HE THONG (CHUYEN SAU)

He thong duoc thiet ke theo kien truc N-Tier (da tang) tren nen platform Spring Boot 3, nham toi uu hoa viec bao tri va mo rong. Thay vi kien truc Monolith truyen thong noi logic bi tron lan, em chia thanh 4 lop tach biet hoan toan:

1. Lop Presentation (Web Layer): Em su dung Spring MVC voi DispatcherServlet lam trung tam. Cac @Controller o day chi dong vai tro "le tan": tiep nhan Request, dung Jakarta Bean Validation de kiem tra dinh dang du lieu ngay tai dau vao, sau do goi xuong tang Service. Em khong cho phep Controller tu y goi truc tiep den Database de tranh lam lo logic truy van ra ben ngoai.

2. Lop Business Logic (Service Layer): Day la noi tap trung toan bo "chat xam" cua du an. Em ap dung nguyen tac Interface-based Programming. Moi Service (nhu CampaignService, DonationService) deu co mot Interface va mot lop Implementation (Impl). Cach lam nay cho phep em trien khai "Loose Coupling" (phu thuoc long leo). Vi du: Neu sau nay chung ta thay doi thuat toan tinh toan tien quy, em chi can thay lop Impl ma khong can sua mot dong code nao o tang Controller. Tai day, em cung quan ly cac giao dich bang @Transactional de dam bao tinh nguyen tu cho du lieu.

3. Lop Data Access (Repository Layer): Em tan dung suc manh cua Spring Data JPA. Thay vi viet cac lop DAO voi hang nghin dong code JDBC thuan, em chi can khai bao cac Interface ke thua JpaRepository. Hibernate se tu dong sinh ra cac cau lenh SQL toi uu. Voi cac truy van phuc tap nhu tim kiem linh hoat User theo nhieu dieu kien, em su dung @Query voi tham so truyen vao de chong tan cong SQL Injection mot cach tuyet doi.

4. Lop Persistence (Database Layer): MySQL 8.0 luu tru du lieu thuc te.

Tai sao em chon kien truc nay? Tac dung lon nhat la kha nang kiem thu doc lap. Em co the viet Unit Test cho lop Service ma khong can phai bat Server hay ket noi Database, giup toc do phat trien nhanh hon va it loi hon.

PHAN 6: CONG NGHE SU DUNG (3 - 4 phut)

Em su dung bo cong nghe hien dai bao gom:
- Java 17: Phien ban LTS on dinh voi nhieu tinh nang moi.
- Spring Boot 3: Giup tu dong hoa cau hinh va trien khai nhanh.
- Spring Data JPA / Hibernate: Quan ly ORM va giao tiep database hieu qua.
- Spring Security OAuth2: De ho tro tinh nang dang nhap bang Google.
- BCrypt: Thuat toan bam mat khau tieu chuan quan doi.
- Maven: Quan ly thu vien va build du an.

Ly do em chon Spring Boot la vi no cung cap Embedded Tomcat, giup em chay ung dung nhu mot file doc lap ma khong can cau hinh server phuc tap, dong thoi he sinh thai cua no rat lon, ho tro tot cho viec bao mat va kiem thu.

PHAN 7: THIET KE CO SO DU LIEU (CHI TIET QUAN HE)

ERD cua he thong duoc chuan hoa den dang chuan 3 (3NF) de loai bo du thua du lieu. Cac bang chinh bao gom:

- Users va Roles: Em thiet ke quan he 1-n. Moi User phai thuoc ve mot Role cu the (ADMIN, USER, GUEST).
- Campaigns: Day la bang trung tam. Em su dung kieu du lieu DECIMAL(15,2) thay vi Double hay Float cho cac truong target_money va current_money. Ly do la DECIMAL luu tru so thap phan chinh xac tuyet doi, tranh duoc sai so "mot vai dong le" khi thuc hien hang nghin phep tinh cong don - mot yeu cau bat buoc trong ung dung tai chinh.
- Donations: Bang nay lien ket 3 thanh phan: User (ai ung ho), Campaign (ung ho cho cai gi) va PaymentMethod (qua kenh nao).
- Companions va Campaign_Companions: Em thiet ke moi quan he n-n (Many-to-Many) thong qua mot bang trung gian. Mot chien dich co the co nhieu doi tac dong hanh (Momo, VTV, Bao Tuoi Tre...) va mot doi tac cung co the tham gia nhieu chien dich.
- User_Following: Luu tru trang thai "quan tam" cua User voi Campaign.

Diem dac biet trong thiet ke: Em su dung khoa ngoai (Foreign Key) voi cac rang buoc ON DELETE CASCADE o cac bang lien ket nhu Donations. Nghia la neu mot chien dich bi xoa (truong hop chua co tien), cac theo doi lien quan se tu dong duoc don dep, giup database luon "sach" va nhat quan.

PHAN 8: THIET KE CHUC NANG CHINH VA GIAI QUYET VAN DE THUC TE

Trong phan nay, em xin trinh bay logic thiet ke cua 5 chuc nang cot loi duoc hien thi theo cac buoc (steps) thuc thi thuc te trong code:

1. Luong Xac thuc va Tai khoan (Authentication & Account Flow):
- Buoc 1: User chon "Dang nhap bang Google". Spring Security redirect nguoi dung den Google Authorization Server.
- Buoc 2: Sau khi User xac nhan, Google tra ve Authorization Code. Spring Security tu dong trao doi Code lay Access Token va lay thong tin UserInfo (Email, Name, Id).
- Buoc 3: OAuth2SuccessHandler trong code se tiep nhan thong tin nay va kiem tra Email trong Database:
  + Case A (Da co tai khoan): He thong cap nhat ProviderId (neu chua co), thiet lap HttpSession (userId, role) va redirect ve Dashboard/Home.
  + Case B (Chua co tai khoan): He thong tam luu Email/Name vao Session va redirect ve trang /register?google=new de User nhap them SDT/Dia chi (dam bao du du lieu nghiep vu).
- Buoc 4: Voi Dang nhap LOCAL, Controller dung BCrypt so khop mat khau hash truoc khi tao Session.
- Buoc 5: SecurityInterceptor dung preHandle de quet URI. Neu vao /admin hoac /user ma Session trong -> redirect ve trang Login ngay lap tuc.
- Buoc 6: Logic "Auto-upgrade": He thong tu dong nang cap GUEST len USER khi ho hoan tat Dang ky hoac Reset Password, giup hop nhat lich su quyen gop.

2. Luong Quan ly Chien dich (Campaign Management Flow):
- Buoc 1: Admin tao/cap nhat chien dich kem theo muc tieu tai chinh (Target Money) va ngay ket thuc.
- Buoc 2: Moi khi du lieu duoc truy cap, ham checkAndUpdateExpiredCampaigns se tu dong quet cac chien dich het han.
- Buoc 3: He thong thuc hien UPDATE trang thai tu IN_PROGRESS sang CLOSED mot cach am tham neu endDate < hien tai.
- Buoc 4: Khi mot khoan quyen gop duoc xac nhan, code tu dong tinh toan ty le hoan thanh. Neu dat muc tieu, status se tu dong chuyen sang COMPLETED.
- Buoc 5: He thong ho tro render thong minh noi dung mo ta chien dich, tu dong bien link anh thanh the <img> truc quan.

3. Luong Quyen gop va Xac nhan (Donation & Confirmation Flow):
- Buoc 1: User/Guest thuc hien quyen gop (Amount >= 1,000 VND). Ban ghi duoc tao voi trang thai PENDING.
- Buoc 2: Admin kiem tra bank/sao ke va bam "Xac nhan" tren Dashboard.
- Buoc 3: DonationService thuc thi ham confirmDonation() trong mot @Transactional duy nhat.
- Buoc 4: Cap nhat trang thai Donation sang CONFIRMED va dong thoi goi CampaignService de cong tien bang BigDecimal (tranh sai so).
- Buoc 5: EmailService tu dong gui Email thong bao xac nhan va loi cam on den nha hao tam ngay khi transaction hoan tat.

4. Luong Quan ly Nguoi dung (User Management Flow):
- Buoc 1: Admin quan ly danh sach thanh vien voi bo loc da nang: Role, Status, Keyword.
- Buoc 2: Chuc nang "Lock/Unlock" cho phep Admin tam ngung quyen truy cap cua tai khoan vi pham.
- Buoc 3: Code trong AdminController co co che "Self-action protection": Chan khong cho phep Admin tu khoa hoac tu xoa chinh minh.
- Buoc 4: He thong ho tro loc nguoi dung khong hoat dong (Inactive) theo tung moc thoi gian (1 tuan, 1 thang...) de Admin de dang quan ly.

5. Luong Theo doi Chien dich va Ho so ca nhan (Tracking & Profile Flow):
- Buoc 1: User bam "Theo doi" chien dich -> Luu vao bang User_Following de nhan cap nhat.
- Buoc 2: Khi vao trang Profile, UserServiceImpl thuc hien aggregation: Tinh tong tien quyen gop, dem so chien dich va so luong theo doi realtime.
- Buoc 3: User co the tu quan ly avatar (upload an toan voi UUID) va thay doi thong tin ca nhan.
- Buoc 4: Trang Profile hien thi lich su quyen gop chi tiet, giup nguoi dung tu minh bach hoa moi giao dich thien nguyen cua minh.

PHAN 9: PHAN QUYEN VA BAO MAT (THUC THI TRONG CODE)

He thong bao mat cua em khong chi la ly thuyet ma duoc thuc thi nghiem ngat qua 3 lop:

1. Ma hoa du lieu nhay cam: Em su dung BCryptPasswordEncoder. Thay vi luu "123456", database se luu mot chuoi ky tu ngau nhien dai 60 ký tu. Diem hay cua BCrypt la no tu dong tao Salt (ma muoi) ngau nhien cho moi lan bam. Nghia la neu hai nguoi dung co cung mat khau la "123456", thi chuoi hash luu trong DB cua ho van hoan toan khac nhau. Dieu nay triet tieu kha nang bi tan cong bang bang tra cuu (Rainbow Table).

2. Phan quyen bang Interceptor (Authorization Layer): 
Em thiet ke lop SecurityInterceptor ke thua HandlerInterceptor. 
- Cach code hoat dong: Trong phuong thuc preHandle, em lay duong dan URI ma nguoi dung dang truy cap. 
  - Neu URI bat dau bang "/admin", em check Session: Neu khong co Object User hoac User do co Role khong phai ADMIN, em se dung luong request ngay lap tuc va redirect ve trang login kem message "Access Denied".
  - Neu URI la "/user/profile", em chi can check xem Session co userId hay khong.
=> Cach nay giup em tap trung logic bao mat vao mot file duy nhat, khong can phai viet if-else kiem tra quyen o tung file Controller.

3. Tich hop Google OAuth2: 
Day la diem nhan cong nghe cua du an. Em cau hinh OAuth2 Client de lay thong tin tu Google API. 
- Logic xu ly: Khi nguoi dung dang nhap bang Google thanh cong, em lay Email tu Google tra ve. Neu email nay da co trong DB, em cho ho vao luon. Neu chua co, em tu dong tao mot tai khoan moi voi auth_provider la 'GOOGLE'. Dieu nay vua tang trai nghiem nguoi dung, vua tan dung duoc lop bao mat 2 lop (2FA) san co cua Google.

PHAN 11: LUONG DEMO THUC TE (10 phut)

Sau day em xin demo mot luong nghiep vu hoan chinh:
1. Dau tien, mot nguoi dung moi se thuc hien quyen gop 100,000 VND cho chien dich "Ao am vung cao".
2. He thong ghi nhan va bao trang thai "Cho xac nhan".
3. Em se dang nhap vao tai khoan Admin, vao danh sach quyen gop de thay giao dich vua roi.
4. Sau khi kiem tra sao ke bank, Admin bam "Xac nhan".
5. Ngay lap tuc, chung ta se quay lai trang chu va thay so tien cua chien dich "Ao am vung cao" da duoc cong them 100,000 VND va thanh tien do da tang len.

PHAN 12: KIEM THU (5 phut)

Em da thuc hien day du cac loai hinh kiem thu:
- Validation Test: Kiem tra cac truong hop nhap lieu sai nhu email sai dinh dang, mat khau qua ngan.
- Service Test (Unit Test): Dung Mockito de test logic tinh toan tien te ma khong can DB.
- Integration Test: Dung MockMvc de test luong tu Controller xuong den Database.
Mot so ca tieu bieu nhu: Chan user thuong vao vung Admin, tu dong dong chien dich khi het han ngay ket thuc deu da Passed.
